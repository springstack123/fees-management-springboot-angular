FEE MANAGEMENT SOFTWARE

Fees management Online is one of the best high school management software programs available to help you manage fees collection through paperless bookkeeping. Eliminate your manual workload with our user friendly school fee management system designed to be easy to use. Whether you are highly experienced or if you have no previous knowledge of accounts, you can easily make changes to fee structures, create fee heads, receive payments and generate fee dues with minimal effort.

You can even define the type of school fees to be collected from a specific grade or create a dynamic fee structure. Fees Management Online is designed to be able to handle many different types of school fee scenarios and will help you to devise custom rules for fees or create new ones based on your unique needs. You can even assign a fee structure to each student automatically based on their class fee structure. All it takes is a few clicks of the mouse and you can print any fee form or receipt within moments when you choose Fees Management Online.

Overview

Easy Customization :
Fees of each student can be auto-calculated based on fee structure, scholarships, discounts etc.
Convenient Payments :
No more long queues at the school office. Round-the-clock online payment options make it easy for staff and parents.
Automatic Reminders & Receipts :
Improve fee collection and enable timely payment with reminders. Auto-generate receipts on every successful payment
Accurate, Transparent Fee Collection :
Eliminate human errors or misappropriation with automatic fee calculation.
Custom Financial Dashboards :
Compile and access data for any time-frame or segment in pre-defined reporting formats in a matter of minutes.
