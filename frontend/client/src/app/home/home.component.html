Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about OnFees

How long does it take to set up OnFees?

Most institutes are up and running within 24-48 hours. We provide dedicated onboarding support to ensure a smooth transition. You can start collecting payments immediately after setup.

Is my data safe with OnFees?

Yes, we use 256-bit encryption and comply with RBI, PCI-DSS, and NPCI security standards. Your data is stored in secure, encrypted servers with regular security audits.

What payment methods do parents have?

Parents can pay via UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and mobile wallets. We support all major payment gateways for maximum convenience.

Can I integrate OnFees with my existing ERP?

Yes! We provide API access for seamless integration with popular ERP systems. Our technical team can assist with custom integrations if needed.

What happens if there's a payment failure?

Our system automatically sends retry notifications to parents. You can also manually trigger reminders. We provide detailed reports on failed transactions.

Do you provide customer support?

Absolutely! We offer email support for all plans, priority support for Professional plans, and 24/7 phone support for Enterprise customers.
